Cresvention 2002!!

Submitted by jen on Fri, 08/02/2002 - 03:00
in
  • Pleasures

Last weekend I attended the 2nd Annual Sifl & Olly "Cres-vention" in Los Angeles. (For those who don't know, "Cres" is short for "Crescent Fresh", the Sifl & Olly way to say "cool".)

The event is an opportunity for hard-core Sifl & Olly fans across the country to to hang out together and spend time with Liam, Matt, and other people responsible for creating the show. It's a conference of silliness, sing-alongs, and general good vibes. I had a fabulous time!

The depth of these folks' knowledge of Sifl and Olly was staggering. You could hold up just about any puppet and everyone could shout out its name (that's "Dynamite Dog and the Worm Who Says WOW!") and recite its respective skit, word for word. The Davis kids, Cole and Teagan, excelled at this particular skill.

For a moment, as I listened to a whole roomful of people singing "I Like Your Spit Because (It's Gravity Defying)", I experienced what could only be described as a "Big Sister Nightmare." Think about the weird things your younger sibling used to say and do. Ok, now imagine that sibling proliferating into a whole ARMY of people enthusiastically talking and acting just like that. Now, realize you're one of them!

Thanks to Greg, Glen and Rachel for the hard work it took to put it all together. Thanks to Matt for his gracious hosting and to Liam for dragging his sick body out of bed to perform for us. Thanks to all you other Sockheads for recognizing the magic in my little brother's work and pursuing it with passion. And thanks, Mom and Dad, for being the true source of all of this.
