The Yellow Belt

This is actually an outtake (and unretouched), but I wanted to post it because we were all so gaga that day over that dress with the yellow belt.

I kept picturing that dress with a shiny, wide, yelllow belt, but of course, I didn't have one, nor did I know where to find one. On a lark, I popped into a shop in Long Beach a few minutes before we left for the studio... and there was the perfect belt just waiting for me!

I like this shot, I only wish it were perfectly symmetrical ('cuz it's so close!).
