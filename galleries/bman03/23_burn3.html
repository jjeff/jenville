Fellow burners

30,000 people look on. [photo by Chris Mandra]

After the Burn, the festivities carry on all night. The Temple is burned the following (Sunday) night and the event officially ends on Monday. Jeff and I packed up the RV and took off early Sunday morning, at about the time most people were just getting back to camp.

Another stunning Burning Man experience comes to an end, as does this photo tour.
