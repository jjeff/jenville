The water truck

Here's a familiar sight throughout the day. In order to keep the dust under control, they regularly water down the roads of Black Rock City. The truck is invariably followed by a troupe of naked guys (and some girls) looking for a quick shower and a cool-down.

This shot was taken from out camp looking out at Dogma Street and the other camps in the distance.
