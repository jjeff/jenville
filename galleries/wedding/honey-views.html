The Honeymoon! We went to a resort/spa in Jamaica called Grand Lido Sans Souci. We wanted total relaxation and not to have to work for anything, and this place certainly delivered. The weather was great, the spa treatments were divine, and we met some fun people. All in all, a very "honeymoony" honeymoon. Perfect. Here are some views of the spa.
