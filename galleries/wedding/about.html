About this site

I designed this site to look and work best on monitors with a resolution of 800x600 or higher. If you have a lower resolution monitor (640x480), you will have to do a lot more scrolling.

Also, the text on this site is formatted with style sheets, which work best on Version 4 web browsers and higher. If you are using an old browser, you will still be able to read the words, but the pages may look awkward (or downright bad!).

Ok, that's all I have to say.
