So, 80s NIGHT!. We danced to Blondie, New Order, the Smiths, and the rest of my college record collection. They also played all the new bands that sound just like 80s bands (Strokes, Interpol, The Faint, etc.). As luck would have it, I learned my dance moves in the 80s, so it was nice to have an opportunity to try them out again.

I had this conversation with the bartender that evening:

BARTENDER: Hey, I saw you dancing. You've got that 80s dancing style down exactly!!

ME: Well.... you know why... right?

BARTENDER: (looking sympathetic) You're... dating yourself?

ME: yes.

BARTENDER: (laughs)

ME: But hey, at least I know all the words to the Interpol and Faint songs as well as the old stuff.

BARTENDER: How do you know all that?

ME: Because I'm cool.

[me dancing; don't know the guy behind me]
