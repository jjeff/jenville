This is the house we stayed in called Pozzarello. It was built in the year 1200 (!) as the home of the gardener who tended the grounds of the adjacent castle. The house has 4 bedrooms, a large living room with fireplace, a decent kitchen and 2 modern bathrooms. With 3-foot thick walls, it stayed nice and cool despite the blistering mid-day heat.
